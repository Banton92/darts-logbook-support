Privacy Policy

Darts Logbook  ·  Last updated: May 3, 2026

Darts Logbook ("the App") is developed and operated by Yannik Bante. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the App and how it is handled.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All notes, setups, goals, and training data are stored locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers by us.

2. Camera & Photo Library

The App may request access to your camera and photo library to let you attach images or videos to your training moments. These files are stored exclusively on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

3. In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time In-App Purchase to unlock the full version. Purchase transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store and by RevenueCat, which we use to manage entitlements. RevenueCat may process anonymised purchase data on our behalf. For details, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy and Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not receive or store any payment information.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected, the App is safe for all age groups in this regard.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the new version will be published at this URL. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
