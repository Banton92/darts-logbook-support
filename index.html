Privacy Policy

Darts Logbook  ·  Last updated: May 16, 2026

Darts Logbook ("the App") is developed and operated by Yannik Bante. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the App and how it is handled.

1. Information We Collect

The App is designed to keep your logbook data on your device. Notes, setups, moments, goals, training observations, and attached photos are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

We do not operate user accounts, analytics, advertising, or tracking systems in the App.

2. Camera & Photo Library

The App may request access to your camera or photo library so you can attach images to setups or training moments, or import setup cards from images. These files are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded by the App.

If you choose to use the share feature, the App creates a share image locally and opens the system share sheet. You decide which app or service receives the shared image.

3. In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time In-App Purchase to unlock the full version. Purchase transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store or by Google through Google Play, depending on where you installed the App. We use RevenueCat to manage purchase entitlements across supported platforms.

RevenueCat may process purchase history and related technical identifiers on our behalf in order to validate purchases, restore purchases, and unlock paid features. We do not receive or store your payment card, bank account, or other payment details.

For details, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy as well as Apple's Privacy Policy and Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses RevenueCat for purchase management. The App does not integrate analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Data Deletion Requests

The App does not create user accounts. You can delete local app data by deleting entries in the App or by removing the App from your device. If you want to request deletion of purchase-related data processed through RevenueCat, contact us at the email address below.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App is intended as a general darts training and setup journal.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the new version will be published at this URL. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
